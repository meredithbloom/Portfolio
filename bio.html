Born in 94' in Boston, but mostly raised in the riveting suburb of Washington DC, affectionately (and actually) known as Mclean, Virginia. I was incredibly shy as a kid, but eventually made friends from playing soccer at recess.
I lived in Brooklyn for a few years after I graduated from Oberlin College in 2016. I really loved it there, but decided moving home to save money during GA was the best move for me. I miss my boxing gym, my friends, and the food, but not the cost of living.


Some fun pics of my family. I have two younger siblings: Ben and Helen, almost-26 and almost-23, respectively. I have a big family and tons of cousins on my Dad's side. Several of these pics are from a family vacation we took last summer to celebrate my Grandpa John's 91st (and 90th, since we were originally supposed to go Summer 2020) birthday. It was the first time all 4 (!) generations of Blooms were in the same place.
I threw in some nice old photos of my parents, plus a pic that I love from my paternal grandparents' wedding.


I'm blessed to have some amazing people in my life.
